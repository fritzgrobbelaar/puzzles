Now, please expand the content of the game. After collecting all the scripts, you will find an ancient spirit which grants you the choice between wizard protector or slinger. After you choose your role, if you chose wizard, you will be able to cast spells and be given the choice between three spells. 1) poison spell - poisons all enemies that will spawn for a limit. and they will immediately drop gems, but not slain. 2) fire spell. this burns your enemies around for 10 seconds. They drop exactly 1 gem for each and they will be slain. 3) speed spell. This gives you 50% quicker movement for 2 minutes. Each spell has a 3 minute cooldown. If you chose protector, you will be given the choice between 3 attacks. 1) the double blade, which will make 2 blade spawn around you, which upgrades your speed stat by 50% for 1 minute and 59 seconds. 2) the auto-kill which slays all enemies that recently spawned. 3) stab. it gives you double range. slaying one enemy will slay another one of the same type. Each attack has a 3 minute and 59 second cooldown.
If slinger was selected. 1) dash, increases speed by 60% for 1 minute and 40 seconds. 2) yeet, which is a range attack in one direction - which slays everything in the same direction. 3) hammer yeet - in a certain radius around the player. The radius is 15% of the screen, around the player, in a circle. that slays everything in this radius. Each attack has a 4-minute and 1 second cooldown.
